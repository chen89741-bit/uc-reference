Data Sources / Disclaimer 数据来源与免责声明

This MVP uses prototype reference data first. It is a decision helper, not an official admissions predictor. 本 MVP 先使用 prototype reference data，是决策辅助工具，不是官方录取预测器。

Overall admit rates and admitted GPA ranges are campus-level references from UC first-year admit data, not major admit rates. 总体录取率和录取 GPA 范围是校区层面参考，不是专业录取率。

Admitted GPA range is not a guarantee. Minimum GPA requirement is separate from admitted GPA range. 录取 GPA 范围不是保证；最低 GPA 要求和录取学生 GPA 范围是两回事。

Do not treat rankings, difficulty labels, or career outcomes as exact. 不要把排名、难度标签或就业结果当成精确结论。

Students should verify majors, requirements, admit data, costs, and outcomes with official UC campus websites. 学生应通过 UC 官方网站核实专业、要求、录取数据、费用和就业信息。

Last updated: June 2026. Data should be rechecked before making application decisions. 最后更新：2026 年 6 月。做申请决定前请重新核实数据。
