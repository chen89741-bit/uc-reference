Data Sources / Disclaimer

This MVP uses prototype reference data first. It is a decision helper, not an official admissions predictor.

Overall admit rates and admitted GPA ranges are campus-level references from UC first-year admit data, not major admit rates.

Admitted GPA range is not a guarantee. Minimum GPA requirement is separate from admitted GPA range.

Do not treat rankings, difficulty labels, or career outcomes as exact. 不要把排名、难度标签或就业结果当成精确结论。

Students should verify majors, requirements, admit data, costs, and outcomes with official UC campus websites.
